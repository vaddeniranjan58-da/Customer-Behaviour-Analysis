# Customer Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Understanding customer behavior is essential for businesses to improve customer satisfaction, optimize marketing strategies, and drive revenue growth. Customer behavior analysis helps organizations identify purchasing patterns, spending habits, and customer preferences, enabling data-driven business decisions.

This project focuses on analyzing customer demographic and purchasing data to uncover valuable insights about customer behavior. Using Python for data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualization, the project identifies trends, customer segments, and factors influencing purchasing decisions.

The goal is to help businesses better understand their customers, improve customer engagement, and support strategic decision-making through actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Businesses collect large volumes of customer data, but extracting meaningful insights from this data remains a challenge. Understanding who the customers are, how they spend, and what influences their purchasing behavior is critical for improving customer retention and increasing profitability.

This project aims to analyze customer behavior data to identify spending patterns, customer segments, and key trends that can help businesses enhance their marketing strategies and customer experience.
